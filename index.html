About Me

I’m Nina Heinemann, a senior at Texas Christian University, double-majoring in Communication Studies and Data Analytics & Digital Culture. I’m interested in how stories, visuals, and data all work together.

I love marketing, digital design, and analytics. I’ve managed social media, helped with branding, experimented with web design, and used data to make projects more strategic. My favorite work is both pretty and practical.

I’m originally from Guatemala, which has shaped my perspective, my style, and my love for color and design. Long term, I’d like to lead a marketing team and eventually build my own brand or business.
