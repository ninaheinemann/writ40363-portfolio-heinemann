About me

I’m Nina Heinemann, a senior at Texas Christian University, double-majoring in Communication Studies and Data Analytics & Digital Culture. My academic journey lets me work at the intersection of communication, media, and technology—building both analytical and creative skills.

I’m passionate about marketing, digital design, and data analytics—and I enjoy bringing these skills together. My experience includes managing social media, developing branding concepts, exploring web design, and using data to shape creative strategies. I’m adaptable and curious, always learning new tools and approaches to create work that looks good, tells a story, and makes an impact.

My long-term goal is to lead a marketing department and eventually establish my own business, where I can combine creativity, strategic thinking, and leadership. Outside of work, I enjoy photography, interior design, pilates, and tennis, and I value time with friends and family.
