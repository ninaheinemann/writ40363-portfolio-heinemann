academic background

I’m Nina Heinemann, a senior at Texas Christian University, double-majoring in Communication Studies and Data Analytics & Digital Culture. My studies allow me to explore the intersection of communication, media, and technology, developing both strong analytical and creative skills.

interests

I’m passionate about marketing, digital design, and data analytics — and I enjoy finding ways to bring these skills together. My experience includes managing social media, developing branding concepts, exploring web design, and using data to shape creative strategies. I bring adaptability and curiosity to every project, always willing to learn new tools and approaches.

goals

My long-term goal is to lead a marketing department and eventually establish my own business, where I can combine creativity, strategy, and leadership skills. Outside of work and academics, I enjoy photography, interior design, pilates, and tennis. I also value time with friends and family, which keeps me grounded and inspired.
